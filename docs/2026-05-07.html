Term of the Day

Yield Curve

A chart plotting interest rates on government bonds across different maturities at a point in time. A normal curve slopes upward — long rates exceed short rates. An inverted yield curve, where short-term rates are higher than long-term rates, has historically predicted recessions and is closely watched by macro investors and central banks alike.
