Term of the Day

KYC – Know Your Customer

Mandatory identity-verification process that financial institutions use to confirm client identity and assess financial crime risk before onboarding. Typical steps include government ID verification, proof of address, and a risk questionnaire. Regulators impose heavy fines for KYC failures, making it a core compliance obligation for banks, brokers, and increasingly crypto exchanges.
