DeFi – Decentralised Finance

Financial services — lending, borrowing, spot trading, yield farming — delivered through smart contracts on public blockchains without traditional intermediaries. Ethereum hosts the majority of DeFi activity. The model is permissionless and non-custodial, meaning no KYC and users retain control of their keys. Smart-contract bugs, liquidity crises, and regulatory uncertainty remain major risks.
