Term of the Day

AUM – Assets Under Management

The total market value of all investments a firm manages on behalf of clients. AUM directly drives fee revenue and is the standard measure of a fund manager's scale and market position. Global asset-management AUM exceeds $100 trillion. Rapid AUM growth can paradoxically hurt performance as large funds struggle to deploy capital without moving markets.
